About Kathy Braznick

My journey and exploration with photography began early in my life. As a young child, I was fascinated by photography. I lived in a home filled with strong visual appreciation for family, animals and nature. Photographic moments capturing remarkable expressions and transformations were highlights of my childhood.

I spent numerous summers in Muskoka and The Kawartha’s cultivating an incredible appreciation for beauty in all things nature. Being fortunate enough to photograph many diverse landscapes and natural settings with ever changing light in all seasons and travelling to places near and far is my greatest passion. There is no bigger sense of accomplishment than bringing those moments home to share with others – this is a great reward.

Expertise
